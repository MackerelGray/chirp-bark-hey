Written by Machina on October 15th, 2024.

Sometimes, as a system, we'll write out memories and things we're doing together in a creative nonfiction sort of way - it's written like fiction, but the events we're writing did actually happen to us.

This bit between Jude and Gavin feels like it's pretty relevant to the way we experience partnership together, as kink partners specifically in this context, and how nonsexual intimacy is affected by having an interspecies (human/android) relationship in headspace where that does affect physical interaction.

We'd greatly appreciate it if you were honest about being an adult before interacting with this. Also, feel free to say this is cute if you want; I think they're adorable, but I'm biased.

Content notes: Swearing, physical touch and vulnerability, some self-deprecating internal judgements, general nonsexual submission and dominance. This is all written as adults doing consensual kink together, and also they're literally holding hands.
